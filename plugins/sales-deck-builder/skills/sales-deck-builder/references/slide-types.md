# Slide Types Catalog

Reference for choosing slide types and building the persuasion arc.

## Persuasion Arcs

### Arc A: Classic Sales (9 slides)

Best for: product pitches, demos, client-facing presentations.

1. **Hook** — Bold claim or provocative question
2. **Problem** — Audience's pain, made vivid
3. **Consequences** — Cost of inaction (money, time, reputation)
4. **Solution** — What you offer, framed as outcome
5. **How It Works** — 3 steps max, visual
6. **Proof** — Numbers, testimonials, logos
7. **Comparison** — You vs. status quo or competitors
8. **Offer** — What they get, stacked for perceived value
9. **CTA** — One action, urgency, zero friction

### Arc B: Investor Deck (10-12 slides)

1. **Vision** — Big market insight
2. **Problem** — Market gap with evidence
3. **Solution** — Product in context of the gap
4. **Product** — Demo/screenshots (image-heavy)
5. **Traction** — MRR, users, growth rate
6. **Market** — TAM/SAM/SOM with sources
7. **Business Model** — Revenue mechanics
8. **Competition** — Positioning map
9. **Team** — Founders + key hires
10. **Financials** — Projections, runway
11. **Ask** — Amount, use of funds, timeline

### Arc C: Internal Pitch (5-6 slides)

1. **Current State** — The problem internally with metrics
2. **Impact** — Cost to team/company
3. **Proposed Change** — What you want to do
4. **Plan** — Timeline, resources, risk mitigation
5. **Ask** — Specific approval or resources

### Arc D: Conference Talk (7-10 slides)

1. **Provocative Open** — Surprising stat or bold question
2. **Context** — Set the stage
3. **Insight 1-3** — Key ideas (1 per slide)
4. **Case Study** — Real-world proof
5. **Takeaway** — What the audience does Monday morning
6. **Close** — Memorable statement + contact

## Slide Type Reference

### Hook / Hero
- **Purpose:** Grab attention in 3 seconds
- **Tailgrids blocks:** hero sections (hero-1 through hero-8)
- **Headline formula:** [Outcome] + [Timeframe or Scale]. "10x Your Pipeline in 90 Days"
- **Max words:** Headline 8, subhead 15
- **Image:** Optional — product hero shot or pure typography

### Problem
- **Purpose:** Make the audience feel their pain
- **Tailgrids blocks:** hero-dark, stats sections, features (repurposed as pain points)
- **Headline formula:** [You] + [negative verb] + [metric]. "Your Team Wastes 12 Hours a Week"
- **Max words:** Headline 10, body 25

### Consequences / Amplification
- **Purpose:** Raise the stakes
- **Tailgrids blocks:** stats with large numbers, CTA blocks (warning tone)
- **Headline formula:** [Metric] + [Lost/Wasted/At Risk]. "$240K/Year in Lost Productivity"
- **Max words:** Headline 8, body 20

### Solution
- **Purpose:** Introduce your offer as relief
- **Tailgrids blocks:** hero with screenshot, features with icon grid
- **Headline formula:** [Verb] + [Outcome] + [Ease]. "Ship Faster Without the Chaos"
- **Max words:** Headline 8, body 25
- **Image:** Product screenshot — the reveal moment

### How It Works
- **Purpose:** Remove complexity fear with 3 steps
- **Tailgrids blocks:** features-3col, steps/process sections
- **Headline formula:** "How It Works" or "3 Steps to [Outcome]"
- **Max words:** 3 step headings (4 words), 3 descriptions (12 words each)

### Proof / Social Proof
- **Purpose:** Build credibility
- **Tailgrids blocks:** testimonials, client logos, stats counters
- **Headline formula:** [Number] + [Credibility]. "Trusted by 2,400+ Teams"
- **Max words:** Headline 8, quotes 20 words each (max 3)
- **Image:** Logos, headshots

### Comparison
- **Purpose:** Position against alternatives
- **Tailgrids blocks:** pricing tables (repurposed), comparison grids
- **Headline formula:** [Old Way] vs. [New Way]
- **Max words:** 5-7 rows, 3-4 words per cell

### Offer / Value Stack
- **Purpose:** Show everything they get
- **Tailgrids blocks:** pricing sections, feature lists with checkmarks
- **Headline formula:** "What You Get" or "Everything Included"
- **Max words:** 8-10 items, 5 words each

### CTA / Close
- **Purpose:** Drive ONE action
- **Tailgrids blocks:** CTA sections, signup blocks
- **Headline formula:** [Action] + [Ease]. "Book Your Demo This Week"
- **Max words:** Headline 8, support 12, button 4

### Data / Metrics
- **Purpose:** Present proof numbers
- **Tailgrids blocks:** stats/counter sections
- **Headline formula:** [Number] + [Meaning]. "4.2x ROI in Q1"

### Team
- **Purpose:** Build trust through people
- **Tailgrids blocks:** team sections
- **Headline formula:** "Built by [Credibility]"
- **Image:** Professional headshots (user provides)
